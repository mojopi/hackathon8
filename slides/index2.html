Your friend, the screwdriver!:

Reminder of what you're doing:

  • In your group, take appliances apart
  • Separate parts into 2 piles cool and not cool (You'll keep the cool parts and put the others aside)
  • Use a screwdriver (and not a hammer) when possible.
  • Put all the "cool parts" you've assembled and put them in the "COOL PARTS" Part of the 14th floor.
  • Put all the "UNcool parts" you've assembled and put them in the "UNCOOL PARTS" Part of the 14th floor.

Break

Part 2:Products/Devices from "Cool" Parts

  • With some brainstorming/design-thinking skills you learned with MSW yesterday, brainstorm devices, products, outlandish fantastical machines, etc. that you could make with your parts.
  • In your groups, make something with your "cool" parts [you can also use hot glue, cardboard, string, etc.].
  • Each of you brainstorm a list of 10 things you could make
  • Each of you make 3 possible mock ups/blueprints for your device. Choose one or two and actually make it!
