Overview (8:20-8:45): Take Apart Day

  • Take apart your group's appliance(s)
  • Separate the parts into "Cool" and "Not Cool"
  • Come up with 10 ideas for product to make out of parts + materials cart
  • Each person in group draw several product ideas
  • Make product
  • Each person make website for product

Materials On Each Table

  • Object for take apart
  • Some tools (e.g. screwdrivers)
  • 1 person from each table: be in charge of ipad to take photographs/videos with, pencils/ paper for brainstorming and blueprints (Rob Q)
