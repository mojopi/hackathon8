Overview of Hackathon

The week begins with a two-day long series of workshops that introduce concepts in and develop competencies around engineering and computer science principles. The students then work in groups with a mentor on a three-day long design challenge that is inspired by the theme of the hackathon. During this phase, students will have the opportunity to apply many of the skills they learned during the workshops, but each project will present a unique set of problems to tackle. On the last day, the students will share their work to their classmates and other members of the Dalton community in a symposium.

The learning objectives for the Hackathon projects are pulled from shared goals between the CS and Engineering Departments. By the end of the experience, students will have practiced:

  • Effectively collaborating with peers and mentors
  • Appropriately using digital and physical tools
  • Working through multiple cycles of an iterative design process
  • Communicating their work to an audience
